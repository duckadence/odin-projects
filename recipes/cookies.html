Cookies Recipe

Description

Cookies are baked or cooked snacks or desserts that are typically small, flat, and sweet. The following recipe will be for a standard chocolate chip cookie. The cookie will be on the flatter side and melts easier due to a large percentage of butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 baking soda
  • chocolate chips/flavoring

Steps

  1. Brown butter and then cool in fridge for an hour.
  2. When chill, mix with sugars till thoroughly combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, salt, and baking soda.
  4. Slowly mix in flour mixture to butter mixture.
  5. When fully mixed, add in preferred flavoring.
  6. Chill in fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Bake in oven at 350Â°F for 12-17 minutes.

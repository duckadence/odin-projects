Milk Tea Recipe

Ingredients

  • 100 mL water
  • 3 g tea leaves
  • 1 tbsp non-dairy creamer
  • 2/3 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water to the temperature needed for the type of tea leaves.
  2. Add the tea leaves in and steep for 5 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea and stir in sugar.
  4. Cool in the fridge.
  5. When cooler than room temperature, add the creamer.

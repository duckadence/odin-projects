Hummus Recipe

Description

Hummus is a Middle Eastern dip, spread, or savory dish made from cooked and mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. This recipe also includes the addition of olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup dried chickpeas
  • 1 tspn baking soda
  • 6 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup + 2 tbsp tahini paste
  • 4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 6 1/2 tbsp ice water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Soak dried garbanzo beans overnight or for 8 hours in a pot of water, making sure the water level is above the garbanzo bean level by 2-4 inches depending on the amount of beans.
  2. Drain water and add water till water level is 2 inches above garbanzo beans and add baking soda.
  3. Bring to boil and let simmer on medium-low for 1 hour.
  4. After 1 hour, keep simmering beans and peel garlic.
  5. Add garlic and lemon juice into a food processor or blender and pulse till garlic is roughly minced.
  6. Let the garlic and lemon juice mixture sit for 10 minutes.
  7. After 10 minutes, drain the garbanzo beans and keep 1/4 cup of the bean water, optionally removing the bean skins.
  8. Blend the garbanzo beans with the garlic and lemon mixture, adding bean water to help blend.
  9. After fully incorporated, add the rest of the ingredients and blend till smooth.
